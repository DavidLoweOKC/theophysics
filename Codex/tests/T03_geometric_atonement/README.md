# Test 3: Geometric Atonement

## What This Tests
Whether the geometric conservation layer breaks without the restorative term and closes again when it is introduced.

## Why It Matters
This is one of the strongest structural tests in the stack.

## Planned Implementation
Tensor/symbolic conservation check around a deficit term and an injected restorative source.

## Pass Criteria
Instability appears without the restorative term and resolves when the term is introduced.

## Fail Criteria
Geometry closes either way, or the restorative term does not repair the deficit.

## Kill Condition
If geometric necessity is absent, the atonement-as-necessity framing needs major revision.
